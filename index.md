# Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 14, 2026

Crazy Dice is a simple dice rolling app.

## Data Collection

Crazy Dice does not collect, store, share, or transmit any personal data or user information.

## Permissions

Crazy Dice does not request any device permissions.

## Advertising

Crazy Dice does not contain advertising.

## Analytics and Tracking

Crazy Dice does not use analytics, tracking tools, cookies, or third-party advertising SDKs.

## Third-Party Services

Crazy Dice does not use third-party services that collect user data.

## Children’s Privacy

Crazy Dice does not knowingly collect personal information from children or from any other users.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if the app changes. Any updates will be published on this page.

## Contact

For privacy-related questions, feedback, or support requests, please open an issue on the Crazy Dice GitHub repository:

https://github.com/diepet/crazy-dice/issues
